## Core Criteria Provenance

### 1. BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #5: customer relevance and implications
- **CIA Kent School**: "Synthesis" (main judgment) is first element, before substantiation
- **FM 6-0**: "Specific recommended action stated upfront"
- **Minto**: "Research bottom-up; communicate top-down. Lead with answer."

### 2. Consumer-Oriented
- **PDB**: "Written in the president's language, not officialese" (Richard Lehman)
- **Amazon Working Backwards**: Customer is hero. "Is the customer clearly defined?"
- **Minto SCQA**: Start where the AUDIENCE is, not where you are
- **Repo principle**: Thinking scaffolding ≠ deliverable content

### 3. Fact-Judgment Separation
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #3: distinguish intelligence from assumptions/judgments
- **FM 6-0**: Separate "pertinent facts" from "assumptions"
- **Heuer ACH**: Structurally separates evidence from interpretation

### 4. Uncertainty and Confidence
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #2: express uncertainties with major judgments
- **IC Confidence Scale**: Low / Moderate / High with explanation
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #1: describe source quality and credibility

### 5. Alternatives Considered
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #4: analysis of alternatives, explain why alternatives less likely
- **Heuer ACH**: Compare competing hypotheses against evidence
- **FM 6-0**: Courses of action with strengths/weaknesses + comparative matrix
- **Minto MECE**: Options mutually exclusive, collectively exhaustive

### 6. Assumptions Stated
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #3: assumptions distinguishable from facts
- **FM 6-0**: "All relevant assumptions" as distinct section
- **Heuer Key Assumptions Check**: Enumerate, assess vulnerability
- **Amazon FAQ**: "Assumptions necessary for success"

### 7. Risks and Failure Modes
- **Amazon FAQ**: "Top failure risks" and "constraints requiring innovative solutions"
- **Heuer Premortem**: Imagine failure, work backward to causes
- **FM 6-0**: Courses of action require "weaknesses"
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #4: consider what would make assessment wrong

### 8. Actionable with Clear Ask
- **CIA Writing** (What/So What/Now What): "Now What" = recommended action
- **FM 6-0**: Decision brief ends with "closing statement requesting approval"
- **Amazon**: PR includes "Getting Started"
- **PDB**: Only "new intelligence warranting attention" — what to act on today

### 9. Logical Structure
- **ICD 203** Tradecraft Standard #6: clear and logical argumentation
- **Minto**: Answer first, same kind rule, summarization rule, logical ordering
- **FM 6-0 ABCs**: Accuracy, Brevity, Clarity

### 10. Prioritized
- **PDB**: Most critical items first. 3-4 items per page.
- **CIA Writing**: Inverted pyramid — decreasing importance
- **Minto Comparative Ordering**: Options ranked by importance
- **Reynolds SNR**: If removable without changing meaning, remove

## Status Brief Evaluation Matrix
